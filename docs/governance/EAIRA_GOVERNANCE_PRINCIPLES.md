## Reality First

Governance begins with what is observed in the repository, candidate record, review artifacts, and supporting evidence. A missing fact is recorded as missing; it is not filled by assumption.

Traceability: GIB-001, GIB-007.

## Evidence Before Design

Evidence must exist before a decision advances beyond review. Minimum evidence includes the observed issue, source of the observation, current workaround when one exists, potential benefits, risks, activation criteria, dependencies, and decision readiness.

Traceability: GIB-001, GIB-007.

## Capability Before Architecture

A capability decision must be reviewable before any later phase is considered. The decision record must show that the capability has sufficient evidence, clear activation criteria, and an explicit decision outcome.

Traceability: GIB-001, GIB-002, GIB-006.

## Architecture Is Never A Starting Point

The reserved `Architecture` status is not used to begin governance work. It can only be considered after a capability decision is approved for that later phase.

Traceability: GIB-002, GIB-008.

## Implementation Requires Approval

The reserved `Implementation` status requires a prior approved governance decision. Candidate, assessment, dry-run, and backlog records do not authorize it.

Traceability: GIB-005, GIB-008.

## Candidate Does Not Equal Milestone

`Candidate` means a subject is proposed for assessment. It does not mean the candidate is approved, scheduled, or included in a milestone.

Traceability: GIB-002, GIB-006, GIB-008.

## Every Capability Needs Activation Criteria

Activation criteria define the evidence-based conditions that must be satisfied before a capability can advance. If criteria are absent, unclear, or unsupported by evidence, the decision should be deferred.

Traceability: GIB-001, GIB-006, GIB-007.

## Every Architecture Must Trace Back To A Capability Decision

Any later use of the reserved `Architecture` status must trace to an approved capability decision. The trace must identify the decision record, approval scope, and evidence basis.

Traceability: GIB-002, GIB-008.
