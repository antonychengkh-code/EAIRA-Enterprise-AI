## Purpose

Provide a consistency checklist for governance documents, candidate records, dry-run reports, and change-review records.

Traceability: GIB-005, GIB-009.

## Scope

This checklist validates consistency and evidence traceability. It does not approve candidate outcomes or revise framework scope.

## Metadata Review

- Required metadata fields are present.
- Version and status are consistent with the review purpose.
- Metadata dependencies identify document relationships.
- Body-level dependencies, when present, describe the subject being reviewed.
- Approval records do not overstate authority.

Traceability: GIB-003.

## Status Review

- Governance status uses the global status model.
- Status entry criteria are satisfied or missing criteria are recorded.
- Candidate status is not treated as approval.
- Deferred decisions identify the missing or unclear decision basis.
- Reserved later-phase statuses are not used without prior approval.

Traceability: GIB-002, GIB-006, GIB-008.

## Reference Review

- Cross-document links resolve.
- Reading order is current.
- Referenced evidence exists.
- Backlog and change-review traces identify the relevant GIB item.

Traceability: GIB-004.

## Terminology Review

- `Governance Review` means review of decision readiness or document consistency.
- `Dry Run` means validation of the governance process, not approval of the candidate subject.
- `Evidence` means observed or cited support in existing records.
- `Deferred` means a decision cannot be made from the available evidence or criteria.

Traceability: GIB-005, GIB-006, GIB-009.

## Governance Review Record

Governance review records should identify reviewed documents, evidence checked, decision outcome or recommendation, and scope limits.

Traceability: GIB-005, GIB-009.

## Dry Run Report Structure

A dry-run report should separate:

- Purpose
- Case
- Observed facts
- Interpretation
- Recommendations
- Findings
- Decision
- Evidence summary
- Framework improvements
- Next revision
- Action items
- Lessons learned
- Change log

Dry-run success means the process was validated and governance improvements were revealed. It does not mean the candidate subject is approved.

Traceability: GIB-009.
